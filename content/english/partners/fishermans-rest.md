Jane & Richard Roche developed Fisherman's Rest Cafe (est.2013) and Catch of the Day Fishmongers (est.2019) on the River Teifi, Aberteifi (Cardigan). The ethos of the setting is to provide sustainable and fresh produce to our customers. Working in collaboration with the Fishing and Coastal Community along the Ceredigion and Welsh Coastline. The heritage, history and the future of the area and environment are key principles of the business.
