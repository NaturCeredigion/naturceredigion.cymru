## Carpentry

Carpentry In our carpentry workshop you can bring projects from home or learn something new. From wood turning to musical instrument making, from upcycling furniture to fixing that old chair, our workshop technician, Harry, is here to help. Use of the carpentry workshop is available for a voluntary donation of £5 half a day, £10 all day, plus the cost of materials.
Green Woodwork
## Green Woodwork
If you've ever fancied a go at the ancient craft of green woodwork, CardiShed is the place. Through the use of pole lathes and shave horses, our tutor Matt can help you make anything from a garden dibber to a Welsh stick chair. Use of the greenwood workshop is available for a voluntary donation of £5 half a day, £10 all day, plus the cost of materials.
Wood Carving
## Wood Carving
Wood carving tuition is available at CardiShed with our skilled volunteer Bob. Perfect for a complete beginner or experienced carver. Carving tuition is available for a voluntary donation of £5 half a day, £10 all day, plus the cost of materials.
