The Biodiversity Team at Ceredigion Nature Partnership are responsible for coordinating the overall health of our wildlife and ecosystems in Ceredigion.

Email biodiversity@ceredigion.gov.uk

Please contact Ceredigion Council if your enquiry is about verge cutting, footpaths, fly-tipping, road safety, dead wildlife, roadkill etc. You can do this via their website, phone 01545 570 881 or email clic@ceredigion.gov.uk

For reporting Rural Crime, please phone the police on 111 or use the Dyfed Powys police website.

For nature incidents, please phone Natural Resources Wales 0300 065 300 or use their website.
